Must Visit Places!

Prithvi Cafe

I have visited this cafe in October 2018 for my birthday!

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you will miss the entrance to the cafe. So be alert enough to spot the black signboard that says "Prithvi cafe" while you are on the main road. The restaurant has a reasonably good menu at reasonable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes can be shared by 2 people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance to avoid disappointments. On other days, the crowd there is pretty hep and happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi theater fest", which you must attend if possible.



Eifel Tower

I visted the Eifel tower in September 2019 for a vacation!

The eifel tower is the center atraction when going to Paris. It is very tall and makes you feel like your flying above the clouds like a bird. Seeing the awesome view from the top the tower is amazing because you can see other attractions like the Louvre and Arc de triumphe! Overall the Eifel Tower is an amzing place to visit!



Buckingham Palace

I visted the Buckingham Palace in October 2019 for a vacation!

Buckingham Palace was a nice place to be at seeing all of the royal designs and statues. The guards did a special performance as they did every morning at around 11 am. All in all buckingham palace is a magnificent place!
